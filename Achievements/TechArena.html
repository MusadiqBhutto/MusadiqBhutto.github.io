Blind Coding Champion – Tech Arena

Victory in the Blind Coding Competition

During my undergraduate studies in B.E. Software Engineering at Mehran University of Engineering & Technology (MUET), Jamshoro, my department’s student society, the Software Engineering Society (SES), organized the annual Tech-Arena event in February 2022 during my second year.

One of the key competitions in the event was the Blind-Coding Competition, where participants were given coding tasks to solve without running or compiling the code. The code had to be written in Notepad and submitted within a given time. Students from various batches of my department, as well as from other IT and computer-related departments, participated in this challenge.

I participated in this competition as part of a team of four, including three of my batchmates. Together, we successfully tackled the given coding challenges and won the competition. Our achievement was recognized by the Vice Chancellor and the Chairman of the Software Engineering Department, who awarded us a shield and certificates for our outstanding performance.
