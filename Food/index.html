Our Story

Crafted with Love,
Rooted in Tradition

Jumani Food Products was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to bring the authentic taste of homemade Pakistani condiments to every household across the country. What started as secret family recipes has grown into a beloved brand trusted by thousands.

Based in Karachi, we handcraft our pickles, spices, and chutneys using fresh, locally sourced ingredients and traditional methods — no shortcuts, no compromises. Every jar is a promise of quality.
